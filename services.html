Thirteen ways we handle the journey.

From the first enquiry to the final return, every part of your travel is something we take care of. Here's the full picture.

01

Flight Bookings & Ticketing

Domestic and international flight bookings for individuals and corporate clients. Fare optimization, multi-stop itinerary planning, group bookings, and last-minute changes — all handled by a single point of contact.

Travel Agency
02

Tour Packages

Local and international tour packages: leisure, adventure, cultural, religious, and educational. Tailored itineraries with curated hotels, guides, and transport, scaled from solo travel to large groups.

Tours
03

Visa Application Processing

Full-service visa support — documentation review, application preparation, advisory on requirements, and embassy interview coaching. Schengen, US, UK, UAE, Canada, and more.

Immigration
04

Airport Transfers & Shuttles

Pickup, drop-off, and shuttle services for travelers and tourists, in Lagos and at destinations worldwide. Punctual, vetted drivers, and tracked bookings.

Ground

Hotel & Accommodation Booking

Hotel reservations, short-term accommodation, and bespoke hospitality arrangements. Negotiated rates with global hotel partners, and the ability to match the right property to your budget and style.

Hospitality
06

Travel Advisory

Policy advisory covering health, luggage loss, trip cancellations, and travel emergencies. We help you understand what's covered, what isn't, and what to do when things go sideways.

Advisory
07

Corporate Travel Management

End-to-end corporate travel — business trips, conferences, executive travel, and full travel-policy management. Dedicated account handling, reporting, and 24/7 support for travelling staff.

Corporate
08

Pilgrimage & Religious Tourism

Hajj and Umrah to Mecca and Medina, pilgrimage tours to Jerusalem, Rome, and other holy sites. Reverent logistics, knowledgeable guides, and group coordination.

Pilgrimage
09

Study Tours & Excursions

Educational tours and excursions for students, professionals, and research groups. School trips, university field studies, and conference excursions — all logistics, no headaches.

Educational
10

Immigration Documentation

Support with work permits, residency permits, and other immigration-related documentation. We walk you through requirements and help prepare clean, complete applications.

Immigration
11

Car Rental

Chauffeur-driven and self-drive vehicle hire for tourists, expatriates, and business travelers. Sedans, SUVs, and executive vehicles in Lagos and across major Nigerian cities.

Ground

Foreign Exchange Support

Intermediary services for travelers needing foreign exchange and currency conversion. We connect you to trusted partners and handle the introductions, so you travel with the right currency in hand.

Financial

Advisory consultations on the best travel routes, providers, and itineraries — for travelers planning ahead or considering options. A clear, expert second opinion before you commit.
