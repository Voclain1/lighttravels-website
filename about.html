A company built on care, detail, and trust.

We are Light Travels, Tours and Logistics Ltd — headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria, serving clients who expect every detail of their journey to be looked after.

Our Story

Built around the people we travel for.

Light Travels was founded on a simple idea — that travel, done properly, should feel effortless to the traveler. From flight bookings and visa processing, to bespoke tour packages and corporate travel management, we have built our practice around handling every detail so our clients can focus on the journey itself.

From our base in Opebi, Lagos, we serve individuals, families, religious groups, students, and corporate clients across Nigeria and beyond. Whether it's a first international trip, a multi-stop business itinerary, a pilgrimage to the Holy Lands, or an adventure to a new continent, we bring the same attention to every booking.

Our Values

The principles that guide every itinerary.

These aren't slogans on a wall. They are the standards we hold each booking, each call, and each client interaction to — every time.

01

Care

Travel is personal. Every itinerary is built around the person taking the trip — their pace, their preferences, their priorities.

02

Precision

Connections, visas, transfers, documentation — the small details are where a trip is made or unmade. We get them right.

03
